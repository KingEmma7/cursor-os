# Decision Log

Append-only record of notable architectural and technical decisions. Newest first. Keep entries short — the point is to remember *why* later, so a future agent (or human) doesn't relitigate or unknowingly reverse a deliberate choice.

Add an entry when you make a decision that is expensive to reverse, surprising, or constrains future work. Don't log routine choices.

## Template

```
## YYYY-MM-DD — <short title>

- **Decision:** what was decided.
- **Context:** the problem and constraints that forced a choice.
- **Alternatives:** what else was considered and why it lost.
- **Consequences:** trade-offs accepted and follow-ups this implies.
```
